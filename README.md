## 1. What we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide and improve the App.

- Account information
  - Name, email address, and profile photo (from Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple)
  - Authentication identifiers (Firebase Authentication UID)
- User content
  - Messages, conversation data, media you upload (images, videos), voice/audio recordings, files
  - Metadata like timestamps and conversation IDs
- Device and app information
  - Device model, OS version, app version, crash/diagnostic logs
  - Push notification token (FCM token)
- Payment information
  - Payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers or CVV. Stripe may collect payer name, email, and limited payment details.

What we do NOT collect:
- Phone contacts, SMS, call logs
- Precise location
- Advertising identifiers (for ad tracking)

Permissions the App may request (purpose-bound):
- Microphone: record audio for voice features
- Camera/Photos: capture or select media to share in the App
- Notifications: send updates and alerts relevant to your activity
- Storage/File access: allow you to select and upload files

## 2. How we use information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account and sessions (Firebase Authentication)
- Deliver core features: conversations, media upload, voice/recording, and real-time updates
- Send transactional and service notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging)
- Secure the App, prevent fraud/abuse, and diagnose issues
- Process payments and subscriptions (Stripe)
- Comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for third‑party advertising or sell your data.

## 4. Data sharing

We share data only with service providers that help us run the App, under appropriate agreements:
- Google/Firebase (Authentication, Firestore database, Cloud Messaging)
- Google Sign-In (OAuth)
- Apple Sign in with Apple (OAuth)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Amazon Web Services S3 (media/file storage)
- Pusher Channels (real-time messaging)

We do not sell personal data. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of our services.

- Payment records: retained per legal and tax requirements.
